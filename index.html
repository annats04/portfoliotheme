CASE STUDY: TRAPHOLT REBRANDING

DURATION: 3 weeks

PERIOD: November - December 2023

TEAM: 4 members

MY ROLE: Creative Director

MY CONTRIBUTION: concept design, brand design, graphic design, UX research, website front-end development

METHODOLOGY: Five-Phase Model [research, strategy, concepts, design, implementation], Design Thinking

METHODS: desk research, survey, interviews, user personas, empathy/affinity/journey maps

TOOLS: Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Figma, HTML, CSS, Github, Miro, Milanote

CREATIVE BRIEF: Develop an updated visual identity for Trapholt museum of art and design, drawing inspiration from the featured artist Nanna Ditzel, with families with children as the main target group.

PROBLEM: Redesigning Trapholt museum's visual identity to appeal to families with kids while maintaining its stature as a serious art institution, necessitating a balance between sophistication and approachability.

SOLUTION:

Crafted a style guide inspired by Nanna Ditzel, considering art museum aesthetics and our target group.

Prioritized facilitating family visits to the museum by creating content that caters to their needs.

Showcased Trapholt as a welcoming and lively cultural hub with interactive activities for all to enjoy.

CASE STUDY: JOB LAUNCHER

DURATION: 5 weeks

PERIOD: November2023

TEAM: 4 members

MY ROLE: UX/UI Designer

MY CONTRIBUTION: UX research, data analysis, low-fidelity wireframes, high-fidelity prototypes

METHODOLOGY: Design Thinking

METHODS: desk research, competitors analysis, survey, interviews, user personas, empathy/affinity/journey maps

TOOLS: Figma, Miro

CREATIVE BRIEF: Design a sleek app for young job seekers, integrating UX research within the design thinking model.

PROBLEM: Making a remote job app for busy students who need to find and apply for remote jobs quickly while also keeping up with their studies.

SOLUTION:

Developed a swipe-based app interface for quick job browsing and application.

Infused the copywriting with a slightly gritty marketing tone, adding a cool vibe to the overall experience, making it unique and memorable

Developed features to cater to student users' needs, such as portfolios and project announcements, for a personalized experience.

Integrated a career counseling feature, providing personalized guidance and support for young professionals.
